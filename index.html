Objective

Random elements will start to appear on the screen. Your job is to destroy every element to reach the next level. The way to destroy these elements is explained above. After every 10 points the difficulty goes up and destroying elements becomes more challenging.

Good luck!

Rules

  1. Destroy the appearing elements.
  2. Destroying an element gives you 1 point.
  3. After every 10 points you go to the next level.
  4. If you fail to destroy an element, you lose a life.
  5. When you reach 0 lives, it's game over.
  6. When you reach 150 lives, you win the game.

Bugs

  1. Hover element can sometimes float off-screen.
  2. (FireFox only): Hover element won't disappear properly (mouseEnter event works different in FF).
